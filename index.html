The Cue Card Room

One minute to prepare · Two minutes to speak · Then the discussion
Ready when you are

You'll get a random cue card, 60 seconds to make notes, then 2 minutes to speak. The recorder stops you at exactly 2:00 — just like the real test — then moves straight into Part 3: five follow-up discussion questions on the same topic. At the end you get one transcript to drop into your Claude IELTS coach for grading.

Best in Chrome or Edge. Your browser will ask for microphone permission — allow it so your speech can be transcribed. Nothing is uploaded; transcription runs in your browser.
