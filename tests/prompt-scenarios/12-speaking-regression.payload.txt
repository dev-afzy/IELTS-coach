CUE CARD (Part 2): Describe a skill you would like to learn.

PART 2 ANSWER (2-min transcript):
So the skill I want to learn is, um, playing the guitar. I have wanted to learn it since I was a child but I never really had the time for it. I think I would learn it by watching videos on YouTube and maybe taking some classes online. It might take maybe one year or two years to become good at it. The reason I want to learn the guitar is because I really like music and I think it is a good way to relax after work. Also my friend plays the guitar and when we go camping he plays some songs and everyone sings together, and I really want to be able to do that too. So yeah, that is the skill that I would like to learn in the future.

PART 3 — FOLLOW-UP DISCUSSION:
Q1: Why do you think some skills are harder to learn as an adult than as a child?
A1: I think it is because adults have less time and also they are more afraid of making mistakes. When you are a child you just try things and you don't really care if you fail, but adults think too much about it.

Q2: How has technology changed the way people learn new skills?
A2: Now it is much easier because you can find everything on the internet. Before you needed a teacher but now there are so many free videos and apps, so people can learn at home in their own time.
