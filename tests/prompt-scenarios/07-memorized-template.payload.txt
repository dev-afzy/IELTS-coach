TASK 2 PROMPT: Some people think that international tourism creates tension rather than understanding between people from different countries. To what extent do you agree or disagree?
TASK 2 ANSWER (268 words):
In this modern era, the issue of international tourism has become a hotly debated topic which attracts considerable attention from all walks of life. While some individuals opine that it creates tension, others are of the firm belief that it fosters understanding. This essay will delve into both perspectives before arriving at a logical conclusion.

On the one hand, it is undeniable that there are a plethora of reasons to support the first view. First and foremost, it is a well-known fact that when people from diverse backgrounds come into contact, misunderstandings can arise. To cite a lucid example, a recent study conducted by a prestigious university revealed that cultural differences can sometimes lead to conflict. Therefore, it is crystal clear that this perspective holds a considerable amount of water.

On the other hand, a considerable segment of society begs to differ on this particular matter. It goes without saying that tourism allows people to broaden their horizons and think outside the box. As the famous saying goes, travel is the best teacher. Hence, it is not far-fetched to conclude that tourism promotes harmony and mutual respect among different nations of the world.

Taking all of the aforementioned points into careful consideration, it can be concluded that although there are two sides to every coin, the advantages of international tourism outweigh its disadvantages by a considerable margin. It is high time the authorities and individuals alike joined hands to make the most of this phenomenon for the betterment of society as a whole and for the generations that are yet to come.
