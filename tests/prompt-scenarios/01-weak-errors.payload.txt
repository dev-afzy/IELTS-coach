TASK 2 ANSWER (289 words):
Nowadays learning foreign language is very important for childrens. In my opinion I am agree that children should start to learn a foreign language in primary school and I will explain my important reasons.

Firstly, when children is young, their brain is more faster to catch new things. If a child learn language in early age, he can speak it like a native and this is very important for his future. For example my cousin he start English in primary school and now he speak very good, but I start in secondary school and I am not speaking good like him. This show that early age is very important.

Secondly, foreign language is important because the world is become more global. Many company want people who can speak two or three language. Also they can make friends from other country and understand other culture which is important thing.

In conclusion, I am strongly agree that learning a foreign language in primary school is very important for the children because of the brain reason and the job reason. Government should make this in all the schools and then all the childrens will get this important benefit in the future.
